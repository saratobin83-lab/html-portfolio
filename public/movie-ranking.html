I first started watching anime when I was a child, but I didn't realize that's what is was until recently. My hero was Rainbow Brite, however, I was unable to pronouce the name (it came out "Rainbow-bow-bow"). I continued my interest in high school; Sailor Moon became a fast favorite.

When my children watched Dragon Ball Z, and Pokemon, those never drew me in the way the first two did. In order to understand their interests, I started watching their other favorite anime shows. I watched My Hero Academia and really enjoyed it. Now I am watching One Piece, and it is funnier than I anticipated.

As I continue to watch more anime, I find myself becoming more immersed in the culture and storytelling. It's a great way to unwind after a long day and explore different worlds and characters.
